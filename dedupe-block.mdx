---
title: "Dedupe Block"
description: "Remove any duplicate information within a list"
---

Have you ever found yourself sending or analyzing data just to later realize the record has duplicated data throughout? This is where the Dedupe Block comes in. The goal here is to better maintain readability and storage while getting rid of any unnecessary dupes. All you must do is insert the text or variable data you would like deduped into the list input field along with the Key you would like deduped in the Key field below. Hit next and watch the magic happen!
