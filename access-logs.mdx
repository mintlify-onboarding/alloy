---
title: "Access Logs"
---

To keep in line with industry best practices, we store access logs for every time a user logs into Alloy. Our access logs track the date a login occurred and the corresponding IP Address.

Access logs cannot be removed. You can find your access logs in the Settings page from the Alloy Console.
