---
title: "Data Centers"
---

Alloy offers two data center options to segregate your data for regional data privacy laws. ISVs can choose between our US and EU data centers.

The US data center is primarily located in the `us-east-1` version (North Virginia) while the EU data center is located in `eu-west-1` (Ireland).

Read more about the [US data center](/docs/us-data-center) or [EU data center](/docs/eu-data-center).
