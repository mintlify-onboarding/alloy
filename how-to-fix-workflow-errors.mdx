---
title: "How to Fix Workflow Errors"
---

If your workflow has any missing fields or errors, you'll see a red warning symbol on the workflow in your dashboard and in the top right in workflow editor.

![2820](/images/4424e29-h1.png "h1.png") 

You will also be able to see the results of your workflow runs from your Workflow Logs panel. You can access this straight from the workflows on your dashboard or inside the workflow editor (see blue highlight).

![2858](/images/0233a38-h2.png "h2.png")
